What is Dashboard as a Service (DaaS)?

DaaS is a cloud-based solution that consolidates data from various sources into a single, easy-to-use dashboard. It enables businesses to track key performance indicators (KPIs) across departments like sales, marketing, and inventory in real time.

Real Impact, Real Stories

National Rural Livelihoods Mission (NRLM):
  • NRLM utilized Dashboard as a Service (DaaS) to implement a Web MIS and mobile app, alongside custom dashboards for Producer Group Grading, Business Planning, and Accounting/Transaction. The pilot across 50 PGs in Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, and Jharkhand streamlined data management and enhanced decision-making for improved performance.
    • Transforming Rural India:
  • TRI leveraged Dashboard as a Service (DaaS) to deliver a Web MIS and mobile app, providing customized dashboards for CLF Business Planning, Loan Management, and Annual Planning. The pilot across 50 Model CLFs in Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, and Jharkhand empowered local communities with enhanced planning, monitoring, and financial insights for stronger institutional development.
    • TATA TRUSTS:
  • Tata Trusts implemented Dashboard as a Service (DaaS) to build a Nutrition Fortification Dashboard, featuring public websites and interactive dashboards that track crop production, nutrient demand, and supply data at the state and district levels. The platform also includes a simulation tool for policy researchers, empowering data-driven decision-making across India.

Stories of change

Our solutions speak for themselves. We strive to generate impact by strengthening the core of social impact organisations through comprehensive Information and Communication Technology solutions (ICT). Our tailor - made solutions have helped 2.5 million end users in the development sector so far, and we are constantly endeavouring for more.
Mahindra Group
  • Tracking 100+ CSR activities across 30 companies, Dhwani's mGrant tool enables Mahindra Group to streamline budget allocation, analyze project impact, and manage employee data efficiently.
    • Sattva Consulting
  • Providing digital learning opportunities to Sattva Foundation’s Bhrata EdTech iInitiative using mForm
    • Transform Rural India Foundation
  • Digitalizing payment approvals, Dhwani’s mForm streamlines the UP Rural Livelihood Mission’s processes for greater efficiency and transparency.
    • Hindustan Unilever Foundation (HUF)
  • Empowering 12 NGOs through its collaboration with Hindustan Unilever Foundation (HUF) to drive sustainable water management using Data Management System to collect and monitor farmer data in multiple languages, enabling informed decisions, effective water resource management, and community resilience.
    • Vedanta
  • Partnering with Vedanta CSR for Nand Ghar initiative to eradicate child malnutrition and improving the health of pregnant women by creatiing profiles of children to track malnutrition and pregnant women’s health.

mFORM
Convert your data into real-time, interactive dashboards and reports

mFORM Web

Our low-code/no-code user-friendly Program MIS software simplifies data collection and monitoring, even in offline environments and across multiple languages. It quickly transforms raw data into real-time insights, enabling informed decision-making. The platform’s project-tracking feature brings everything into one place, from dashboards and survey builders to easy data migration and training sessions, making it ideal for project assessments and audits (such as Need, Baseline, Midline, Impact, SRoI). Additionally, the platform integrates seamlessly with mGrant, offering extended value and functionality to streamline your grant management process.

mFORM Mobile

The mobile version of mForm is specifically designed for collecting and monitoring data on-the-go. Whether you’re in the field conducting surveys or training sessions, mForm provides an easy-to-use platform for tracking activities and monitoring the progress of your beneficiaries.

You can collect and monitor data quickly, offline, accurately, in multiple languages and at scale, providing valuable insights into program performance.

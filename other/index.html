mLearn
mLearn is a dynamic, do-it-yourself platform tailored to meet your specific learning needs. Designed to deliver, track, and document e-learning courses, mLearn offers a customizable solution for creating engaging multimedia content, tracking progress, and setting personalized learning paths. Whether you're scaling up for larger groups or customizing content for specific users, mLearn ensures that learning is efficient, engaging, and scalable for all.
mLearn is a mobile-based Learning Management System (LMS) designed for cadre teams like ASHAs, ANMs, Bank Sakhis, and Pashu Sakhis. This highly configurable tool enables NGOs to quickly deliver, track, and document educational courses. Through standardized content delivery, assessments, and progress tracking, NGOs can ensure consistent training across all teams. The platform also provides insights into trainee behavior, helping identify areas for improvement.
